About Me

I began animating a few years ago as a way of expressing myself. I've always been pretty interested in film and media, and I wanted to create my own opportunities. That DIY spirit is at the heart of my work, shaping my films and keeping me experimenting.

While probably not particularly in vogue right now as a reference, I often see myself as a young Dennis Waterman. Opportunities come and go, but I'm ultimately looking for the big one: total creative control for the total creative package, only then will I write, act, produce—and yes, sing the theme tune.
